# Online Queue Management System – Business Analysis Project

## Project Overview

This project analyzes and designs an **Online Queue Management System for outpatient hospital services**.

The goal of the system is to reduce waiting time for patients and improve hospital workflow by allowing patients to **take queue numbers remotely and schedule medical appointments through a mobile application**.

The system integrates with the hospital’s existing patient management system to synchronize medical records and appointment data.

---

## Business Context

Currently, the hospital outpatient process is handled manually:

* Patients must come to the hospital to take a queue number.
* They wait in multiple stages:
  Queue registration → Payment → Waiting for doctor consultation.
* The hospital already has a patient management system but does not support remote appointment booking.

This process leads to long waiting times and inefficient patient flow management.

---

## Problem Statement

### For Patients

* Long waiting time at the hospital
* Uncertainty about when their turn will arrive
* Time wasted during registration and payment

### For Receptionists

* Manual queue number distribution
* High risk of mistakes when patient volume is high

### For Doctors & Hospital Management

* Difficulty predicting the number of patients per department
* Hard to allocate medical staff effectively

---

## Proposed Solution

The project proposes expanding the hospital mobile application with new features:

* **Remote queue number booking**: Patients can take queue numbers before arriving at the hospital.
* **Appointment scheduling**: Patients can choose a doctor and available time slot.
* **System integration**: Synchronization with the existing hospital information system for patient records and appointment history.
